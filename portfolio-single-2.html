E-commerce Bookstore

A conversion-driven bookstore theme tailored for independent authors and boutique publishers.

Technology Html, Css, Javascript, Bootstrap, jQuery, Php, Mysql
Timeline 6 Weeks
My Role Self-Developer, Designer
Live Demo BookStore.netlify.app

Project Overview

This store was designed for a boutique publishing collective that needed a flexible, story-first shopping experience. I created a Shopify theme powered by modular sections so editors can launch new landing pages without touching code. The experience highlights featured authors, subscription boxes, and limited-edition bundles while keeping the checkout frictionless.

Key Features
  • Reusable content sections for new book launches
  • Smart product bundles powered by metafields
  • Author spotlight pages with newsletter capture
  • Optimised theme assets for sub-second loads
Results
  • 36% uplift in add-to-cart rate within the first month
  • Reduced bounce rate on campaign launches by 28%
  • Streamlined content updates via drag-and-drop sections
  • Integrated Klaviyo flows to nurture new subscribers
